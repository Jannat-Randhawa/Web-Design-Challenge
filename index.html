Summary: Latitude Vs X

The aim of this project was to analyze how the proximity to the equator impacts weather changes. In order to demonstrate this analysis, we queried in weather data of more than 500 cities from the OpenWeatherMap API.

To perform a more methodological analysis we used matplotlib to organize and graph different data. We compared 4 factors against latitude. These factors were; max temperature, humidity, cloundiness and wind speed. Lastly, we gather all the data, explanations and visualizations from the analysis and displayed them on this website.
